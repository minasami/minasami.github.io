Operating loop

Six leadership moves

1. Assess readiness

Clarify whether the initiative is ready to fund, pilot, scale, repair or redesign.

Run assessment →

2. Record the decision

Capture assumptions, evidence, risks, owner and review date before momentum hides weak thinking.

Open journal →

3. Execute in stages

Translate the decision into a 90-day delivery path with ownership and governance checkpoints.

Open playbook →

4. Track benefits

Measure value after go-live instead of confusing activity with impact.

5. Inspect proof

Review evidence, claim boundaries and technical delivery signals before strengthening public claims.

6. Learn and improve
