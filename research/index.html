Research & Innovation

Areas I am actively studying, testing and applying across healthcare operations, health informatics, medicine access and responsible digital transformation.

Research themes

Questions that connect healthcare, technology and delivery

Health Informatics

How can information systems improve continuity, coordination and decision-making in real healthcare operations?

Focus areas include data structures, workflow design, implementation, interoperability concepts and the human factors that determine adoption.

Medicine Access Operations

How can medicine support programs become more transparent, sustainable and measurable?

Areas include beneficiary workflows, pharmacy coordination, procurement, fulfillment visibility, budget tracking and partnership models.

AI-Assisted Operations

Where can AI reduce administrative burden without replacing clinical judgment?

Potential applications include document checks, summaries, prioritization, medicine matching, reporting assistance and operational decision support.

Impact Measurement

Which metrics genuinely reflect service quality and continuity?

Interests include coverage, turnaround time, medicine continuity, geography, demand patterns, service performance and program outcomes.

Responsible innovation

Principles guiding the work

Human oversight

AI should support qualified people, not silently replace clinical or operational accountability.

Evidence before claims

Features, outcomes and professional credentials should be described accurately and without overstating evidence.

Usability matters

A system only creates value when real users can understand it, trust it and adopt it in everyday work.
