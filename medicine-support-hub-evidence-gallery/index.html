Sanitized screenshot summary

What the provided screenshots support

1. Public platform surface

The live app presents Medicine Support Hub as digital health infrastructure for medicine access, with clear positioning around requests, beneficiaries, programs, budgets, fulfillment, and measurable impact.

2. Admin dashboard

The admin area shows operational structures for organizations, programs, beneficiaries, timeline events, enterprise data, and user-role administration.

3. Organization model

The administration interface includes organization creation fields, organization type selection, location fields, contact fields, notes, and active status indicators.

4. Role-based access concept

Screenshots show roles and account controls, supporting the claim that the platform is designed around differentiated access and administration.

5. Operational data structures

Visible product structures include programs, beneficiaries, timeline events, organization members, and workflow records.

6. Cloud delivery signal

The app is accessible through a Vercel production URL and is positioned with Supabase RLS foundation and deployment evidence.
