Seven-layer model

Digital health must be managed as an operating system

Every layer affects the others. Weak workflow design damages adoption. Weak ownership damages governance. Weak data damages measurement. Weak sustainment kills value after go-live.

1

Strategic intent

Define the healthcare, operational or access problem. Tie it to measurable value, not vague digital transformation language.

2

Real workflow

Map roles, decisions, handoffs, exceptions, escalation, delays and current workarounds before discussing features.

3

Governance

Define executive sponsor, clinical owner, operational owner, technical owner, stage gates and decision rights.

4

Data and controls

Clarify required data, privacy needs, audit trail, reporting, role-based access, data quality and integration points.

5

Technology delivery

Build or configure around the workflow. Validate with real scenarios, not only happy-path demos.

6

Adoption system

Prepare SOPs, training, support, champions, communications, behavior change and removal of old duplicate processes.

7

Sustainment and value

Run post-launch reviews, incident tracking, user feedback, benefits measurement and continuous improvement.

Role clarity

The four owners every digital-health initiative needs

Executive owner

Owns strategic priority, funding, decisions, escalation and value realization.

Clinical owner

Owns clinical logic, safety, review criteria and professional acceptability.

Operational owner

Owns daily workflow, staffing, SOPs, service quality and adoption.

Technical owner

Owns architecture, configuration, integrations, security, releases and support.

PMO rhythm

The weekly leadership rhythm

Decision review

What decision is blocked, who owns it and when will it be made?

Risk review

Which risks threaten safety, adoption, timeline, quality or benefits?

Workflow review

What is the system learning from users, exceptions and incidents?

Adoption review

Who is using it, who is not, and why?

Value review

What changed compared with the baseline?

Next action

What is the next concrete action, owner and date?
