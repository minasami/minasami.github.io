Creator & Architect

My Contributions to Medicine Support Hub

As the founder, clinical architect, and lead PMO professional behind Medicine Support Hub, I spearheaded the platform end-to-end—from initial clinical discovery and system architecture to full-stack development and cloud deployment at medicinesupport.app/medicines.

1. Domain Translation & Clinical Workflow Architecture

Bridged the gap between clinical pharmacy practice and software engineering by leveraging my background as a licensed pharmacist. Modeled real-world chronic medicine eligibility rules, therapeutic substitution protocols, clinical reviewer verification checks, and multi-tier approval gates.

2. 86,000+ Egyptian Medicine Database Integration

Engineered and indexed a comprehensive database of over 86,000 Egyptian pharmaceutical products, complete with brand names, active ingredients, dosage forms, and therapeutic categories. Enabled instant bilingual search (Arabic/English) and automated alternative suggestions for out-of-stock items.

3. Role-Based Governance Model (8 Roles)

Designed and built granular role-based access control (RBAC) across 8 distinct user personas: Requesters, Clinical Reviewers, Physicians, Pharmacists, Procurement Officers, Branch Managers, Delivery Agents, and System Administrators—ensuring strict clinical auditability and data governance.

4. Full-Stack Engineering & Production Cloud Deployment

Engineered the platform using React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and Supabase (PostgreSQL with Row-Level Security policies). Architected the CI/CD pipeline, database schemas, REST APIs, and production cloud deployment hosted live at medicinesupport.app/medicines.

Strategic Horizon

My Vision for Medicine Support Hub

Medicine Support Hub was created to transform chronic healthcare access across Egypt and the MENA region by replacing fragmented manual routines with an open, audit-ready, and scalable digital health operating system.

Universal Access & Health Equity

Empower NGOs, Patient Support Programs (PSPs), and public health providers to ensure no chronic disease patient faces treatment interruption due to paper bottlenecks or supply fragmentation.

End-to-End Operational Transparency

Provide program leaders and funding partners with real-time financial tracking, budget utilization dashboards, inventory forecasting, and verified impact analytics.

AI-Assisted Decision Intelligence

Pave the way for AI-enabled clinical triage—incorporating optical character recognition (OCR) for medical prescriptions, smart drug-drug interaction alerts, and automated demand forecasting.

The problem

Medicine support programs are often fragmented

Requests can move across spreadsheets, messages, paper forms, pharmacy teams, physicians, procurement staff and delivery partners without one source of truth. That creates delays, duplicated effort, weak reporting and limited accountability.

Disconnected intake

Requests arrive through multiple channels with inconsistent documentation and follow-up.

Clinical complexity

Medicine alternatives, chronic therapy requirements and reviewer decisions need structured handling.

Limited visibility

Managers struggle to see workload, fulfillment, budget utilization, geography and impact in real time.
