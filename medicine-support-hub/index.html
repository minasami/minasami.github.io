Medicine Support Hub

Medicine Support Hub is a digital health operations platform concept designed to help organizations manage chronic medicines support workflows with better transparency, accountability, reporting and coordination.

Open live platform

Vision & Mission

Vision

To make medicine support programs more transparent, sustainable and measurable.

Mission

To give healthcare support organizations one structured operating system for requests, review, budgets, fulfillment tracking and reporting.

Features

Request workflow

Structured intake, documentation status, reviewer assignment and request tracking.

Role dashboards

Separate views for operations, review, pharmacy, branch and administration teams.

Reporting

Operational dashboards for volumes, status, utilization, budget visibility and follow-up.

Partner workspace

Organization profiles, program ownership, case management and stakeholder visibility.

Procurement tracking

Supplier lists, purchase planning, fulfillment status and cost visibility.

Impact tracking

Coverage, continuity, geography and program-level outcomes reporting.
