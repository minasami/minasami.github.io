# Platform Roadmap

This roadmap keeps the career platform focused. The goal is not to add pages forever. The goal is to improve leverage.

## Current platform strengths

- Clear digital-health positioning.
- Executive, recruiter, PMO and CEO routes.
- Proof Room and Claims Registry.
- LinkedIn Content Engine and Outreach Command System.
- Growth Center and Career Intelligence Dashboard.
- Quality Gates, Runbook, PR template and issue templates.

## Next priorities

### 1. Strengthen the homepage

Make the first screen simpler, sharper and more executive. The homepage should route visitors quickly to the Growth Center, Command Center, Executive Brief and Proof Room.

### 2. Improve case-study depth

Medicine Support Hub and Employee Support Program should continue to get stronger with clearer structure:

- problem
- constraints
- action
- system
- measurable impact
- proof boundaries
- next conversation path

### 3. Build search visibility

Publish fewer but stronger insights around:

- digital-health implementation
- healthcare PMO
- workflow adoption
- operating models
- governance rhythms
- post-go-live sustainment

### 4. Improve conversion paths

Every major page should move to one of these actions:

- contact Mina
- start an executive conversation
- run a readiness assessment
- review proof
- share the platform
- send targeted outreach

### 5. Measure useful attention

Track the signals that matter:

- clicks to Executive Brief
- clicks to Proof Room
- clicks to Contact
- outreach replies
- recruiter or executive conversations
- useful LinkedIn traffic

### 6. Keep claims current

Update the Claims Registry, Proof Room and `llms.txt` whenever a fact changes, especially around credentials, project scope, role scope or evidence.

## Avoid list

Do not add pages that do not improve:

- proof
- clarity
- distribution
- conversion
- measurement
- trust

Do not broaden the brand into random productivity, generic leadership or unrelated technology topics. The focused lane is digital health, healthcare PMO, workflow transformation, adoption and execution.
