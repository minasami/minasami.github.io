Framework

The seven-part proof structure

1. Problem

What was fragmented, slow, risky, manual, unclear or hard to measure?

2. Constraints

What realities shaped the work: people, tools, data quality, adoption, governance or budget limits?

3. Mina’s role

What did Mina coordinate, design, document, automate, report or lead?

4. System design

What workflow, roles, data, dashboards, controls or escalation paths were created?

5. Evidence

What numbers, artifacts, screenshots, documents or working systems support the claim?

6. Boundaries

What should not be overstated: sole ownership, budget control, certification or clinical authority?

7. Next conversation

What should a recruiter, CEO or PMO leader ask next?
