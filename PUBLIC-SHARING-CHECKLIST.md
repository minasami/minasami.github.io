# Public Sharing Checklist

Use this before sharing the platform on LinkedIn, with recruiters, or with potential partners.

## Before sharing
- Review the homepage.
- Review the Executive Digital Health Tour.
- Review the Medicine Support Hub case study.
- Review the Proof Review Trail.
- Review the Claims Registry.
- Confirm that wording stays evidence-based.

## What to share confidently
- Digital health product thinking.
- Healthcare workflow design.
- Implementation and governance logic.
- Evidence discipline.
- Learning and gamified leadership experience.

## What not to overclaim
- Clinical production use.
- Enterprise validation.
- External adoption.
- Certification.
- National-scale deployment.

## Strongest next proof
- Redacted screenshots.
- Short demo with sample data.
- Architecture snapshot.
- Testing or usage notes when available.
