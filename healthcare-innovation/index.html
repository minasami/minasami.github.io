Innovation thesis

The opportunity

Healthcare support programs often fail operationally before they fail clinically. The gap is usually workflow ownership, data visibility, escalation, procurement discipline, funding transparency and implementation adoption. Mina's work focuses on turning those gaps into structured, measurable systems.

Access

Design workflows that help eligible patients reach chronic medicines more reliably.

Govern

Use PMO discipline to make ownership, risks, status and escalation visible.

Measure

Build analytics around continuity, coverage, cost, demand and service performance.
