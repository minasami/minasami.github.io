# Maximum Result Criteria

This document defines what “maximum result” means for Mina Tawfik's digital-health career platform.

The goal is not infinite pages. The goal is a platform that produces the right conversations with the right people.

## Maximum result means

1. A new visitor understands Mina's positioning within 10 seconds.
2. A recruiter can find role fit, proof and contact paths without confusion.
3. A CEO or founder can understand the digital-health transformation value quickly.
4. A skeptical reviewer can verify claims and boundaries.
5. Mina has a weekly operating rhythm for content, outreach and follow-up.
6. The site has automated checks that prevent broken quality.
7. Future work is prioritized by roadmap, not impulse.

## Required system layers

- Entry point: homepage and Command Center.
- Decision layer: Career Intelligence Dashboard.
- Growth layer: Growth Center, LinkedIn Engine, Outreach System and Share Hub.
- Proof layer: Proof Room, Claims Registry and case studies.
- Conversion layer: Executive Brief, Conversation Kit and Contact.
- Governance layer: Quality Audit, Quality Gates, Runbook, validation workflow and templates.
- Roadmap layer: Platform Roadmap and repository ROADMAP.md.

## What is fulfilled now

The platform now has the core layers needed for serious career growth:

- positioning
- proof
- routing
- distribution
- outreach
- conversion
- quality governance
- roadmap discipline

## What should still improve over time

The next improvements should be selective:

- sharpen the homepage copy
- deepen case studies
- improve search-focused insight content
- review analytics signals
- keep claims current
- improve conversion paths based on real user behavior

## Decision rule

If a proposed change does not improve proof, clarity, distribution, conversion, measurement or trust, do not build it yet.
