Value evidence

Benefits tracking dimensions

Adoption

Are users actually using the new workflow in the intended way?

Operational performance

Did cycle time, handoffs, rework, backlog or service reliability improve?

Financial value

Are cost, resource use, procurement, waste or productivity assumptions improving?

Quality and safety

Are errors, delays, missed escalations or unmanaged risks decreasing?

Patient or beneficiary experience

Did access, clarity, responsiveness or trust improve for the people served?

Governance maturity

Are leaders reviewing evidence and making better decisions at each stage gate?

Weekly

Adoption risks, blockers, workflow issues and incident themes.

Monthly

KPI movement, benefits evidence, resource use and decision log updates.

Quarterly

Scale, repair, redesign or stop decisions based on accumulated evidence.

Sustainment ownership, value protection and lessons learned.
