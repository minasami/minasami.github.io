Avoid these claims

Wording that should not be used without new evidence

Avoid: “Owned a 120M EGP budget”

Better: “supported governance, coordination, reporting and portfolio visibility across healthcare support programs exceeding EGP 120M.”

Avoid: “PMP certified”

Better: “PMP certification in progress.”

Avoid: “Clinical-trial leader”

Do not claim clinical-trial leadership unless a validated project is added.

Avoid: “SAP/S4 certified”

Do not claim ERP certification without proof.
