Review questions

What to ask while reviewing

For recruiters

Which role does this prove: digital-health PM, healthcare PMO, operations transformation, or technical delivery?

For executives

What business problem does the workflow solve, and what operational visibility does it create?

For PMO leaders

Where are governance, ownership, escalation, reporting and adoption visible?

For product teams

How does the case study translate real healthcare work into roles, flows, data and controls?
