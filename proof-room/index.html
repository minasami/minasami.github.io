What should not be overstated

No inflated budget ownership

Use “supported governance, coordination, reporting and portfolio visibility across healthcare support programs exceeding EGP 120M,” not “owned a 120M budget.”

No completed PMP claim yet

PMP status should remain “in progress” until credential evidence is updated.

No clinical-trial leadership claim

Do not imply clinical-trial leadership unless evidence is added later.

No unsupported enterprise ERP certification

Do not claim SAP/S4 certification unless documentation is added.
