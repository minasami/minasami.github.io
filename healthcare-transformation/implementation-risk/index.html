Implementation Risk in Healthcare Transformation

Implementation risk is the possibility that a change will not be delivered, adopted or sustained as intended.

Common risk areas

  • Unclear ownership
  • Insufficient capacity
  • Weak workflow design
  • Low readiness
  • Dependency failure
  • Data or integration problems
  • Inadequate support after launch

Management cycle

  1. Identify risks early.
  2. Assess likelihood and impact.
  3. Assign one owner.
  4. Define a response.
  5. Set escalation thresholds.
  6. Review until closure.
