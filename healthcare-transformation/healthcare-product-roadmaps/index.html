Healthcare Product Roadmaps

A healthcare product roadmap connects outcomes, user needs, capabilities and delivery sequencing.

Roadmap elements

  • Outcome themes
  • User problems
  • Capabilities
  • Dependencies
  • Evidence and assumptions
  • Measures

Good practice

  1. Organize around outcomes.
  2. Keep dates proportional to confidence.
  3. Show dependencies.
  4. Review assumptions.
  5. Update using evidence.
