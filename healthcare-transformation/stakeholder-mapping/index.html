Stakeholder Mapping in Healthcare Transformation

Stakeholder mapping identifies who is affected by change, how much influence they have, what they need and how they should be engaged.

Core groups

  • Executive sponsors
  • Clinical and operational leaders
  • Frontline users
  • Patients and service users
  • Technical and data teams
  • Partners, vendors and regulators

Practical method

  1. List every affected group.
  2. Assess influence, impact and current support.
  3. Identify concerns and expected benefits.
  4. Assign an engagement approach.
  5. Review the map as the program changes.

Common mistake

Do not focus only on senior decision-makers. People with less formal authority may determine whether the new workflow is adopted.
