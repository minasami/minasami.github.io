# Healthcare Service Design

Healthcare service design improves the complete experience across people, processes, information and technology.

## Core activities

- Understand user needs
- Map the current journey
- Identify friction and duplication
- Co-design the future service
- Test with users
- Measure outcomes

## Useful outputs

- Journey map
- Service blueprint
- Future-state workflow
- Role map
- Measurement plan
