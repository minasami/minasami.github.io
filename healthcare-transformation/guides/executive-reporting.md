# Executive Reporting for Healthcare Transformation

Executive reporting should support decisions rather than summarize activity.

## Core questions

- Are outcomes and milestones on track?
- What is at risk?
- Which decisions are blocked?
- Where is capacity constrained?
- Are expected benefits appearing?

## Recommended sections

1. Outcome summary
2. Portfolio status
3. Top risks and issues
4. Dependencies and decisions
5. Adoption and benefits
6. Actions required from leadership
