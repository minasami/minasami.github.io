Healthcare Pilot Planning

A pilot tests whether a proposed change works in a controlled setting before wider rollout.

Pilot components

  • Clear hypothesis
  • Defined users and location
  • Entry and exit criteria
  • Training and support
  • Measures
  • Decision rules

Practical steps

  1. Define what must be learned.
  2. Select a representative setting.
  3. Set limits and safeguards.
  4. Prepare users and support.
  5. Collect evidence.
  6. Decide whether to adapt, expand or stop.
