Healthcare Transformation Framework

This framework connects strategy, governance, delivery, adoption and measurable value.

  1. Diagnose: define the problem, baseline, stakeholders and constraints.
  2. Design: define the target operating model, workflows and capabilities.
  3. Prioritize: rank initiatives by value, readiness, dependency and capacity.
  4. Mobilize: confirm sponsorship, governance, roadmap and measures.
  5. Deliver: execute through project, product or hybrid methods.
  6. Adopt: prepare users, train teams and reinforce new behaviors.
  7. Realize benefits: measure operational change and outcomes.
  8. Learn and scale: improve, standardize and expand what works.
