Telehealth Implementation Guide

Telehealth implementation connects service design, technology, workflows, training and measurement.

Core workstreams

  • Service scope
  • User eligibility
  • Scheduling
  • Clinical and operational workflow
  • Technology and support
  • Training
  • Measurement

Implementation steps

  1. Define the service and target users.
  2. Map the end-to-end journey.
  3. Design escalation and exception routes.
  4. Prepare technology and support.
  5. Pilot with a limited group.
  6. Measure access, experience and outcomes.
