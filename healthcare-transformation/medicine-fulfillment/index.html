Medicine Fulfillment Operations

Medicine fulfillment operations coordinate approved requests, sourcing, order status, delivery and confirmation.

Core workflow

  1. Receive an approved request.
  2. Confirm medicine details and quantity.
  3. Check availability.
  4. Coordinate supplier or pharmacy action.
  5. Track exceptions and delays.
  6. Confirm delivery and close the request.

Useful measures

  • Turnaround time
  • Fulfillment rate
  • Stock-related delays
  • Exception volume
  • Delivery confirmation
  • Continuity of support
