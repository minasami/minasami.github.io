Healthcare Program Governance

Program governance creates clear forums, roles and decision routes for coordinated delivery.

Core components

  • Executive sponsor
  • Steering forum
  • Program leadership
  • Workstream ownership
  • Decision routes
  • Escalation thresholds

Review rhythm

  1. Review outcomes and milestones.
  2. Resolve cross-team dependencies.
  3. Escalate material risks.
  4. Confirm decisions and owners.
  5. Track benefits and adoption.
