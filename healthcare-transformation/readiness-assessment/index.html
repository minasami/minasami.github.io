Healthcare Change Readiness Assessment

A readiness assessment evaluates whether leadership, people, workflows, technology and support are prepared for change.

Assessment areas

  • Leadership alignment
  • Stakeholder support
  • Workflow clarity
  • Capability and training
  • Technology readiness
  • Data readiness
  • Support capacity

Practical steps

  1. Define the proposed change.
  2. Assess each affected group.
  3. Identify barriers.
  4. Assign corrective actions.
  5. Reassess before launch.
