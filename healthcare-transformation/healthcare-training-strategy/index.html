Healthcare Training Strategy

Training should prepare each role to perform the new workflow safely and confidently.

Training design

  • Role-based learning objectives
  • Real workflow scenarios
  • Practice and feedback
  • Job aids
  • Super-user support
  • Post-launch reinforcement

Practical process

  1. Map affected roles.
  2. Define required behaviors.
  3. Assess current capability.
  4. Design role-specific learning.
  5. Practice before launch.
  6. Measure performance after launch.
