SOP Governance in Healthcare Transformation

Standard operating procedure governance keeps critical workflows clear, current, approved and usable.

Core controls

  • Named document owner
  • Version control
  • Approval record
  • Review date
  • Change history
  • Training and acknowledgment

Lifecycle

  1. Draft from the real workflow.
  2. Validate with users.
  3. Approve through the correct authority.
  4. Publish one controlled version.
  5. Train affected roles.
  6. Review after process changes or incidents.
