Healthcare Adoption Measurement

Adoption measurement shows whether people are using a new process, product or operating model as intended.

Measure more than training

  • Usage
  • Workflow completion
  • Error and rework
  • User confidence
  • Support requests
  • Manager reinforcement
  • Outcome improvement

Practical method

  1. Define the target behavior.
  2. Choose leading and lagging indicators.
  3. Establish a baseline.
  4. Review by user group.
  5. Investigate low adoption.
  6. Act and remeasure.
