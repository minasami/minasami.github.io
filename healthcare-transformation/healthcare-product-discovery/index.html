Healthcare Product Discovery

Product discovery reduces the risk of building the wrong healthcare solution.

Discovery questions

  • Who has the problem?
  • How is it solved today?
  • What evidence shows the problem matters?
  • What constraints affect the solution?
  • What outcome should improve?

Practical process

  1. Define the problem.
  2. Interview users and stakeholders.
  3. Map the current journey.
  4. Identify assumptions.
  5. Test concepts.
  6. Choose the smallest useful experiment.
