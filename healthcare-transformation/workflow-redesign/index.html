Healthcare Workflow Redesign

Workflow redesign improves how people, information and systems work together to deliver a service.

Redesign steps

  1. Define the outcome.
  2. Map the current workflow.
  3. Identify delays, duplication and unclear handoffs.
  4. Design the future workflow.
  5. Test with representative users.
  6. Measure performance after launch.

Useful outputs

  • Current-state map
  • Future-state map
  • Role and handoff matrix
  • Exception routes
  • Measurement plan
