# Healthcare Transformation Quality Review Checklist

## Definition
- Problem and intended outcome are clear.
- Target users are identified.
- Scope and exclusions are documented.

## Delivery
- Owners and decision routes are defined.
- Dependencies are visible.
- Risks and issues have actions.
- Acceptance criteria are agreed.

## Adoption
- Affected groups are mapped.
- Training and support are prepared.
- Adoption measures are selected.

## Benefits
- Baseline and target are defined.
- Benefit owner is named.
- Post-launch review is scheduled.

## Evidence
- Claims are supported.
- Limitations are explicit.
- Review date is recorded.
