Healthcare Journey Mapping

Journey mapping shows how a person experiences a service from first contact through completion and follow-up.

Include

  • Goals
  • Touchpoints
  • Waiting
  • Handoffs
  • Information needs
  • Problems
  • Outcomes

Method

  1. Select one service journey.
  2. Collect input from users and staff.
  3. Map the current experience.
  4. Identify friction.
  5. Design improvements.
  6. Pilot and measure.
