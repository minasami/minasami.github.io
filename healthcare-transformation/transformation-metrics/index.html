Healthcare Transformation Metrics

Transformation metrics should show whether delivery, adoption and outcomes are improving.

Balanced categories

  • Patient and service outcomes
  • Operational performance
  • Workforce adoption
  • Financial value
  • Digital usage
  • Risk and quality

Good measurement practice

  1. Establish a baseline.
  2. Define targets.
  3. Name measure owners.
  4. Separate leading and lagging indicators.
  5. Review trends, not isolated numbers.
  6. Act on the evidence.
