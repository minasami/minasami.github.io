Change Fatigue in Healthcare Transformation

Change fatigue appears when teams face more change than they can absorb effectively.

Warning signs

  • Low participation
  • Repeated delays
  • Declining confidence
  • Training overload
  • Workarounds
  • Manager inconsistency

Response

  1. Review the total change load.
  2. Sequence initiatives.
  3. Protect frontline capacity.
  4. Remove low-value activity.
  5. Strengthen local support.
  6. Measure readiness and adoption.
