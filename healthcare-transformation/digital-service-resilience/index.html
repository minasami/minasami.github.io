Digital Service Resilience in Healthcare

Service resilience helps healthcare teams continue critical work during technology, supplier or operational disruption.

Core areas

  • Critical service identification
  • Fallback workflows
  • Recovery priorities
  • Supplier dependencies
  • Communication routes
  • Testing and learning

Practical cycle

  1. Identify critical services.
  2. Map failure points.
  3. Define fallback processes.
  4. Assign recovery ownership.
  5. Test realistic scenarios.
  6. Improve from lessons.
