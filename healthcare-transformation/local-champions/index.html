Local Champions in Healthcare Transformation

Local champions help teams understand, test and adopt a new way of working.

Champion responsibilities

  • Share local feedback
  • Explain the change
  • Support practice
  • Identify barriers
  • Escalate recurring issues
  • Reinforce adoption

Good practice

  1. Select respected people from affected teams.
  2. Clarify the role.
  3. Provide early access and training.
  4. Create a regular feedback route.
  5. Recognize contribution.
