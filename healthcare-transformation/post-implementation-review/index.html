Post-Implementation Review in Healthcare

A post-implementation review checks whether the delivered change is stable, adopted and producing the expected value.

Review areas

  • Outcome performance
  • User adoption
  • Operational stability
  • Open risks and issues
  • Benefit progress
  • Lessons learned

Review steps

  1. Compare results with the baseline and target.
  2. Gather user and operational evidence.
  3. Review unresolved issues.
  4. Confirm benefit ownership.
  5. Assign improvement actions.
  6. Record reusable lessons.
