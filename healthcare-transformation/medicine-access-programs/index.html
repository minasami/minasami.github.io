Medicine Access Program Design

Medicine access programs connect eligible people with reliable, governed support for obtaining required medicines.

Core components

  • Eligibility criteria
  • Request intake
  • Clinical and administrative review
  • Pharmacy and supplier coordination
  • Delivery and confirmation
  • Data, reporting and escalation

Design principles

  1. Define the target population.
  2. Standardize required evidence.
  3. Clarify decision ownership.
  4. Design exception pathways.
  5. Measure access, turnaround and continuity.
