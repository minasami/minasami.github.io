Benefit Ownership in Healthcare Transformation

Benefit ownership assigns accountability for turning delivered outputs into measurable outcomes.

Owner responsibilities

  • Confirm the baseline
  • Agree the target
  • Enable required operational changes
  • Review evidence
  • Address barriers
  • Confirm realization

Practical steps

  1. Define the expected benefit.
  2. Name one accountable owner.
  3. Identify enabling changes.
  4. Set measures and review dates.
  5. Track performance after launch.
