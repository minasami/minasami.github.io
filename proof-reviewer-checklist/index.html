Reviewer checklist

Use this before trusting the case study

1. Product evidence

  • Is there a live app or working surface?
  • Are real workflows visible?
  • Are admin or operational structures visible?
  • Are screenshots sanitized before public use?

2. Technical evidence

  • Is there a connected GitHub repository?
  • Is there a hosted deployment?
  • Is the system database-backed?
  • Are architecture claims specific and bounded?

3. Governance evidence

  • Are roles and permissions represented?
  • Are organizations, programs or workflows structured?
  • Are audit or timeline concepts visible?
  • Are decision and escalation ideas documented?

4. Claim discipline

  • Does the case avoid overclaiming production use?
  • Does it separate evidence from ambition?
  • Does it state what is not yet proven?
  • Does it route reviewers to proof pages?
