Executive readiness diagnostic

Digital Health Readiness Assessment

Score each statement based on the current state of the initiative. This is not a vanity maturity score. A low score in one dimension can expose a critical weakness that should stop or reshape funding, pilot or scale decisions.

Interpretation

How executives should use the output

85–100

Scale-ready. Prepare controlled scale with governance, benefits tracking and sustainment ownership.

70–84

Pilot-ready. Run a governed pilot with named owners, success criteria and adoption controls.

50–69

Funding-risk. Repair weak foundations before major funding, procurement, build or rollout.

0–49

Redesign required. Fix the operating model before technology decisions dominate.
