Privacy Notice

This page explains what information may be collected when you visit minasami.github.io and how that information is used.

What this website collects

This portfolio does not ask visitors to create accounts or submit sensitive personal information. When you contact Mina through email, WhatsApp or LinkedIn, the information you provide is handled by those services according to their own privacy policies.

Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics 4 with measurement ID G-RWM4XN0FL5 to understand general usage patterns such as page views, navigation paths and interactions with major links. IP anonymization is enabled in the site configuration.

Analytics data is used to improve the website, understand which professional content is useful and identify broken or underperforming pages. It is not used to sell personal data.

Tracked interactions

The site may record events such as opening project case studies, clicking LinkedIn, GitHub, WhatsApp or email links, using the recruiter assistant, printing the recruiter profile and encountering a missing page.

These events describe website activity. They are not intended to collect confidential information.

Cookies and browser controls

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar browser technologies. You can restrict cookies through your browser settings, use private browsing, enable tracking protection or install an analytics opt-out extension.

Your choice: blocking analytics should not prevent you from reading the portfolio, project pages or contact information.

External services

This website links to third-party services including GitHub, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Replit, Google and QuickChart. Opening those services may allow them to collect information under their own terms and privacy policies.

Data retention and access

Analytics retention is managed through the connected Google Analytics property. Visitors who contact Mina directly may request correction or deletion of information they previously provided, subject to legitimate record-keeping needs.
