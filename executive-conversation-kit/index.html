Meeting agenda

A focused 30-minute conversation

1. Context

What problem are you trying to solve: healthcare operations, digital adoption, PMO setup, workflow transformation, hiring or advisory?

2. Current state

What currently exists: manual workflow, system, dashboard, team structure, backlog, adoption issue or transformation initiative?

3. Success definition

What must improve: turnaround, visibility, adoption, quality, risk, service experience, governance or executive reporting?

4. Evidence review

Review Mina’s proof: Medicine Support Hub, ESP transformation, operating-system map and claims registry.

5. Fit discussion

Discuss whether the need matches Mina’s strongest zones: digital health, healthcare PMO, workflow transformation and implementation support.

6. Next step

Agree on a concrete next action: interview, advisory call, diagnostic review, project scoping or follow-up material.

Questions to bring

Make the meeting useful

For CEOs and founders

  • Where is our digital-health initiative stuck: workflow, ownership, adoption, reporting or technology?
  • What evidence should we require before scaling?
  • What operating model would reduce execution risk?

For recruiters

  • Which role is closest to Mina’s evidence: digital health, PMO, healthcare operations or technical delivery?
  • What claims need verification before presenting him to leadership?
  • Which case study best supports the target role?

For PMO leaders

  • What dashboards, governance rhythms and escalation paths are missing?
  • What should executives measure beyond go-live?
  • Which project controls would improve delivery confidence?

For healthcare operations teams

  • Where are handoffs, exceptions and delays happening?
  • What old process must stop after digitization?
  • How will users be trained and supported after launch?
