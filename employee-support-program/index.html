Healthcare transformation case study

Employee Support Program Transformation

Transformation of a fragmented, manual employee healthcare support process into a structured ticket-based operating model with clearer ownership, automation, dashboards, SOPs, escalation and user training.

ServiceDesk PlusStructured case and ticket management
Google Apps ScriptAutomated routing from Google Forms
Cross-functionalHR, People Operations, reviewers, pharmacy and IT
Governed deliveryKPIs, SOPs, dashboards and escalation
Business challenge

Manual processes were limiting visibility and consistency

Requests moved through forms, email, messages and individual follow-up. That made ownership unclear, delayed escalation and made it difficult to monitor workload, service status and recurring operational problems.

Before

  • Manual intake and follow-up
  • Fragmented communication
  • Limited status visibility
  • Dependency on individual memory
  • Difficult reporting and escalation

After

  • Structured ticket-based intake
  • Automated routing and assignment
  • Centralized status tracking
  • Defined ownership and service stages
  • Dashboards, SOPs and escalation pathways

My contribution

Bridging healthcare operations, users and technology

Process design

Mapped the real workflow, clarified stages, ownership, responsibilities and escalation paths.

Automation

Innovated an integration between Google Forms and ServiceDesk Plus using Google Apps Script to automate routing while preserving a simple user experience.

Dashboarding

Created centralized operational views for request handling, medicines, alternatives, pricing and follow-up.

Governance

Developed SOPs, role guidance, reporting structures, KPIs and implementation controls.

Training

Designed and delivered system and duty training for review teams and operational stakeholders.

Stakeholder coordination

Acted as a liaison across People Operations, HR, reviewers, pharmacy, technology teams and leadership.

Key deliverables

Evidence of structured implementation

Workflow maps

Clear stages, ownership, handoffs and exceptions.

SOPs and runbooks

Repeatable guidance for system use and operational duties.

KPI dashboards

Workload, status, pending actions and operational visibility.

Automated routing

Reduced manual handling between intake and ticket creation.

Training materials

Role-based guidance supporting adoption and consistency.

Escalation pathways

Visible handling for urgent, blocked and exceptional requests.
