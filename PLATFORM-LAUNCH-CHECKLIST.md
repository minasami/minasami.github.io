# Platform Launch Checklist

Purpose: define the final review steps before actively sharing the digital health platform.

## Core pages
- Home page
- Executive Digital Health Tour
- Digital Health Leadership Operating Loop
- Digital Health Experience Map
- Learning Lab
- Medicine Support Hub Case Study
- Proof Review Trail
- Claims Registry

## Proof readiness
- Claims remain bounded.
- Evidence pages are public-safe.
- Screenshots are redacted before publishing.
- Demo walkthrough uses sample data only.

## Technical readiness
- Reliability roadmap exists.
- Security hardening roadmap exists.
- Known limits are documented.
- Vercel runtime access still needs proper permission.

## Launch rule
Share the platform confidently as a portfolio-grade digital health leadership and product-thinking demonstration. Do not claim clinical production use, enterprise validation, certification, external adoption or national-scale deployment without stronger public evidence.
