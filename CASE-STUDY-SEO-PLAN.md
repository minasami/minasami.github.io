# Case Study SEO Plan

Case studies should support both trust and discovery.

## Primary search themes

- digital health case study
- healthcare PMO case study
- healthcare workflow transformation
- medicine support platform
- chronic medicine support workflow
- healthcare operations automation
- pharmacy workflow platform
- patient support program operations

## Priority pages

### Medicine Support Hub

Search angle:

- digital health platform
- medicine support workflow
- pharmacy operations platform
- role-based healthcare workflow

Improve over time with:

- clearer screenshots
- workflow diagrams
- evidence notes
- implementation lessons
- adoption and governance sections

### Employee Support Program

Search angle:

- healthcare support workflow
- employee medical support process
- healthcare operations transformation
- PMO governance for support programs

Improve over time with:

- before and after workflow
- stakeholder map
- service stages
- escalation model
- dashboard and reporting examples

## Content rules

- Write for recruiters and executives first, search engines second.
- Use precise healthcare operations language.
- Keep claims tied to proof and boundaries.
- Avoid generic buzzwords unless the page explains what they mean operationally.

## Internal links

Every case study should link to:

- Case Study Proof System
- Proof Room
- Claims Registry
- Executive Conversation Kit
- Contact

## Decision rule

Do not publish search content unless it also improves proof, clarity, conversion, or trust.
