Scorecard

Rate each proof item

1. Clarity

Can a reviewer quickly understand what the evidence shows and why it matters?

2. Safety

Is private or sensitive information removed before the evidence is shared publicly?

3. Workflow proof

Does the evidence show an actual process, not only a static screen?

4. Technical proof

Does the evidence connect to a live product, repository, database-backed structure or deployment signal?

5. Governance proof

Does it show roles, permissions, review steps, ownership or decision structure?

6. Claim discipline

Does the caption state what the proof supports and what it does not prove yet?

Decision rule

Only publish strong evidence.

1-2: keep private or improve first.

3: publish with careful wording.

4: strong enough to support a stronger portfolio claim, if the claim remains bounded.
